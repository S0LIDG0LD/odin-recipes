Orignal Bolognese Lasagna

Description

Recommended ingredients for 6 people
  • 4 eggs
  • flour
  • mince
  • tomato sauce
  • grated cheese
  • olive oil
  • milk
  • salt
  • water

Steps:

  1. Mix water with eggs and flour into a dough
  2. Use a pasta machine anche to squeeze the dough into layers
  3. Pan fry tomato sauce with oil and salt
  4. Pan fry the mince with oil
  5. Layer by layer put a pasta layer with mince and tomato sauce
  6. Cover the last layer with grated cheese

Lasagne origins from the Region of Emilia in the Center-North of Italy. It is a traditional recipe served as first course.

Lasagne should be served warm but not too hot as coming from the owen it keeps a lot of heat for long time.
